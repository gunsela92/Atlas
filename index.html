What Is Mecellem

Produced by New Mind Ltd, Mecellem is an integrated and smart platform for management of legal knowledge and services. It is designed to include 16 different products, employing various techniques of artifical intellgience such as natural language process, machine learning, blockchain, to offers solutions for different categories of needs of both legal -service providers and receivers.
Mecellem stands out with its sui generis eco-system, in which all legal-tech features are interacting with each other in an integrated manner in a one-stop solution and it is unique in the sense that it works artificial legal intelligience in Turkish language as well.

Why Mecellem

The first Turkish legal codex in modern context was produced in 1868 by some Turkish lawyers as a response to the receiptionist movements. Redesigning the traditional and local law with modern techniques, language and classification, the first modern codification was regarded as native, yet, innovative and transformative.
Our products also are emboydment of the same vision of ‘ innovative, native and standard-transforming’ characteristics that Mecelle carried out in Turkish legal history, in the area of legal-technology today, and Mecelle represents the first native software codex and platform in Turkish legal sector for management of legal knowledge and services in a digital and smart way.
In order to address the continutiy of the same vision in Turkish legal practise, this platform is named as Mecellem.

Products

Mecellem products are designed fully based on real sector expectations and feedbacks and developed with various artifical intelligence techniques as multilayered and in interaction with each other. They can therefore work both in integration with each other and as an independent unit.

Mukavele
Contract Management and Contract Due Diligence System

Enabling the lawyers and client companies to manage all their contracts through one stop, Mukavele produces automated wholistic or customized analysis in relation to contracts, maps the related contracts and documents and more importantly it analyses the provisions of the contracts and produces a due diligence report in seconds, in a comprehensive classification of the rights and liabilities by various criteria.

Muhakemat
Smart Deal with Litigation

This product classifies all data and documents of your litigation files through artificial intelligence and allows you to see all phases of the cases in one window; make analysis of the all cases in a wholistic and integrative way or in accordance with your selected criteria; and visualize the analysis digitally and produces brief executive reports.

Mahfuz
A Smart, Secure and Integrated Document Management System

It combines a data-room service with a highly comprehensive document management system that includes a artificial intelligence- based archiving, classification, versioning, document sharing, data and document security, permission features and it integrates with other Mecelle products for maximum efficiency and effectiveness.

Muamelat
Legal Project Management

This product offers a wholistic Legal Project Management tool through which you can follow up and manage all phases of the Project at one stop solution and provide the highest effectiveness.

Malumat
Swift Knowledge, Sound Decision

It is a unique product of a legal database with over 100 million classified documents and a customized search engine, with advanced capabilities of NER, MWE and OCR. It offers lawyers a great opportunity to do their research in the fastest way possible in a highly improved accuracy.

Müellif
Think Smart, Draft Fast : A Document Automation Tool

It is an automation tool that , through diligent questionnaries, allows one to produce or reproduce all kinds of legal documents and contracts based on a highly rich library of contracts and clauses of all types. It is designed to maximize time-and-cost efficiency as well as the quality and comprehension of the documents

Mahsusat
Personal Data Protection with Smart Redaction

It offers a smart solution for the corporate world to manage Personal Data Protection responsibilities and allows to store and share all documents through automated redaction in accordance with PDP Law.

Mahir
Smart Way to Manage Contracts

Dissecting the contracts into clauses and converting it into a smart document, it integrates the executable clauses with business process systems and follows their execution automatically.

Mülk
Due Diligence for Real Estates

It analyzes the documents of the real estates which are digitilly identified to the system in accordance with real estate and related laws as well as applicable administrative decisions, extracts the phrases which contain risks and produce an overall due diligent report for real estates.

Smart Compass for Document Management

Mursit is a unique product that merges a very comprehensive legal experience and expertise with artificşal intelligence and it identifies and categorizes thousands of various legal document types in an ontological map of legal meaning and practice for the most effective use of documents.
